Welcome to Operational Assurance Group

Operational Assurance Group are a trusted and well-established consultancy across the North East of England, recognised for the comprehensive delivery of Safe Systems of Work (SSoW) and industrial risk control measures. Since our establishment in 2025, we have been proud to deliver innovative, practical solutions to clients from our base in the heart of the UK's industrial landscape—Teesside.

While we operate nationwide, we maintain a strong focus on supporting organisations throughout the North East. Whether engaged on a contract, sub-contract, or one-off basis, Operational Assurance Group offers an extensive portfolio of services delivered by experienced industry professionals. Our team brings the knowledge and expertise required to provide highly specialised, efficient, and reliable operational support. Read on to learn more.

Our Comprehensive Portfolio of Specialist Services

Our expertise extends across a broad range of operational risk and compliance disciplines, allowing us to support clients at every stage of their operations. We provide tailored, high-quality documentation and consultancy services designed to meet the specific needs of each organisation.

All deliverables are fully aligned with UK legislation, HSE guidance, and recognised industry best practice, while remaining flexible to incorporate client-specific standards, procedures, and expectations. Our focus is always on delivering practical, compliant, and effective solutions that support safe, efficient, and audit-ready operations.
