Trusted Safety & Risk Management Experts

Operational Assurance Group is a UK-based consultancy specialising in the development and implementation of Safe Systems of Work (SSoW) and industrial risk control solutions.

Established in 2025 and operating from Teesside, we support organisations across the UK, with a strong presence in the North East. We work with clients on a contract, sub-contract, and project basis—delivering practical, compliant, and reliable safety solutions tailored to complex operational environments.

Our team combines industry experience with technical expertise to provide high-quality documentation, consultancy, and on-site support that keeps people safe and operations running efficiently.

Specialist Services That Support Safe, Compliant Operations

We provide a comprehensive range of services designed to manage risk, ensure compliance, and improve operational performance. All solutions are tailored to your specific requirements and aligned with UK legislation, HSE guidance, and recognised industry best practice.

Our focus is simple: to deliver clear, effective, and audit-ready systems that work in the real world.
