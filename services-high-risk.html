High Risk & Specialist Activities

Specialist documentation for high-consequence operations

Overview

High-risk activities require detailed planning, robust documentation, and competent supervision. We specialise in producing permit systems, rescue plans, and safe systems that satisfy regulatory requirements and protect workers. Our high-risk activity consultancy covers working at height, confined space entry, hot work, electrical isolation, lifting operations, and pressure systems.

These activities are subject to specific regulations including the Work at Height Regulations, Confined Spaces Regulations, LOLER, and PSSR. Our documentation ensures compliance while providing clear, practical guidance that can be implemented by site teams under time and operational pressure.

Consultancy Services Available

We develop permit to work systems tailored to the complexity and risk profile of your operations. Whether you need a simple hot work permit or a comprehensive multi-permit framework for a major shutdown, we design systems that control risk without creating unnecessary bureaucracy.

Our rescue planning services ensure that emergency response capabilities are in place before high-risk work begins. From confined space rescue to working at height emergency procedures, we help clients prepare for low-probability, high-consequence scenarios.

What We Provide

We work with project teams and site management to develop permit to work systems and specialist procedures tailored to your operational context. Our high-risk documentation is designed to control hazards effectively while remaining practical for implementation under time and operational pressure.

From rescue planning to atmospheric monitoring procedures, we ensure your organisation is prepared for high-consequence activities and that emergency response capabilities are in place before work begins.
