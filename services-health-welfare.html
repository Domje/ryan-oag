Occupational Health & Welfare

Occupational health risks are frequently underestimated. We help clients address both physical and psychological health hazards, meeting their duty of care under UK law and best practice guidance. From COSHH assessments and manual handling evaluations to stress risk assessments and welfare facility planning, we ensure your organisation protects the long-term health of your workforce.

We conduct thorough occupational health risk assessments that consider exposure levels, task duration, environmental factors, and vulnerable worker groups — supporting ongoing monitoring and health surveillance programmes. Increasingly, psychological wellbeing and mental health are recognised as critical components of workforce health; we help clients assess work-related stress, develop support frameworks, and create workplace cultures that protect mental health alongside physical safety. From hazardous substance exposure assessments to vibration and ergonomic evaluations, we deliver practical solutions that support your duty of care under UK law.
