Our Approach

A structured, practical methodology for delivering safety excellence

At Operational Assurance Group, we believe that effective safety management is built on understanding your unique operational context. Our approach combines deep industry knowledge with practical, implementable solutions that protect people while enabling confident operational delivery.

Our Methodology

A proven process for delivering audit-ready, practical safety solutions

1

Understand

We begin by thoroughly understanding your operational context, risk profile, and business objectives. This includes site visits, stakeholder interviews, and review of existing documentation to ensure our solutions are tailored to your specific needs.

2

Assess

Our consultants conduct comprehensive risk assessments, gap analyses, and compliance reviews. We identify areas requiring attention and prioritise actions based on risk significance and regulatory requirements.

3

Design

We develop bespoke safe systems of work, procedures, and documentation that are practical, usable, and audit-ready. Every document is designed for real-world application, not just filing away.

4

Implement

We support the rollout of new systems and procedures, providing training, briefings, and ongoing consultation to ensure your team understands and can effectively use the documentation we provide.

5

Review

Safety is an ongoing commitment. We offer periodic reviews, updates, and continuous improvement support to ensure your systems remain current, compliant, and effective as regulations and operations evolve.

Our Core Principles

The values that guide everything we do

Safety First

Every recommendation we make prioritises the protection of people. We never compromise on safety for convenience or cost.

Practical Solutions

Documentation designed to be used on the ground, not filed away. We create systems that work for real people in real operations.

Compliance Focus

Every deliverable meets UK health and safety legislation and industry best practice, ensuring you are audit-ready at all times.

Partnership Approach

We work alongside your team, not above them. Our consultants integrate with your operations to deliver solutions that fit your culture.

Responsive Delivery

Clear Communication

Technical expertise communicated in plain language. We ensure your team understands what we deliver and how to use it effectively.

What Sets Us Apart

Industry-Specific Expertise

Unlike generic safety consultancies, we specialise exclusively in construction, chemical, and petrochemical sectors. Our consultants have direct operational experience in these environments, understanding the unique challenges, terminology, and regulatory frameworks that apply.

Audit-Ready Documentation

Every document we produce is structured for regulatory scrutiny. Clear version control, traceability, and alignment with HSE guidance ensure your documentation stands up to inspection and supports professional tender submissions.

Scalable Support

From single-site projects to multi-year framework agreements, we scale our support to match your needs. Whether you need a one-off risk assessment or ongoing consultancy, we deliver consistent quality at every level.

Continuous Improvement

We stay current with evolving HSE guidance, industry best practice, and emerging risks. Our clients benefit from up-to-date, defensible documentation that reflects the latest regulatory expectations and operational learning.
