Operational Excellence & Reliability

Some operations require bespoke documentation and consultancy that goes beyond standard frameworks. Our Operational Excellence service draws on deep sector knowledge to support complex, non-routine, or high-consequence tasks — including turnaround and shutdown planning, simultaneous operations (SIMOPS), major incident response, and safety case support.

We work with operations and maintenance teams to standardise work processes and build reliability into systems. Whether you are planning a major petrochemical shutdown or developing Standard Operating Procedures to reduce downtime and improve team consistency, we deliver practical tools tailored to your operational context — from PPM schedules and Root Cause Analysis to Management of Change implementations.

Services Available

  • GG02 - Planned Preventative Maintenance (PPM) schedules
  • GG03 - Root Cause Analysis (RCA) investigation & reporting
  • GG05 - Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) implementation
  • GG06 - Work Instruction (WI) implementation
  • GG07 - Management of Change (MOC) implementation
