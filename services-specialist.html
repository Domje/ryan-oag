Operational Excellence & Reliability

Standardising work, reducing downtime, improving reliability

Overview

Some operations require bespoke documentation and consultancy that goes beyond standard frameworks. Our specialist activities service draws on deep sector knowledge to support complex, non-routine, or high-consequence tasks. This includes turnaround and shutdown planning, simultaneous operations (SIMOPS), major incident response, and safety case support.

These activities are often project-critical and carry significant operational, safety, and financial consequences. Our consultants bring direct experience in managing and documenting these complex scenarios, ensuring that planning is rigorous, control measures are effective, and documentation stands up to regulatory and client scrutiny.

Consultancy Services Available

Our specialist activities consultancy is tailored to each client's unique operational context. Whether you are planning a major petrochemical shutdown, coordinating simultaneous operations on a live facility, or developing a safety case for a high-hazard installation, we provide the expertise and documentation to support confident delivery.

We also support clients with bespoke training material development, technical authority frameworks, and document control systems. Our specialist service is where deep sector knowledge meets operational reality, delivering solutions that work under pressure.

What We Provide

We work with operations and maintenance teams to standardise work processes and build reliability into systems. Our documentation supports planned preventative maintenance programmes, process improvements, and operational continuity.

From Standard Operating Procedures to Management of Change implementations, we deliver practical tools that reduce downtime, improve team consistency, and drive operational excellence across your facility.

  • GG02 - Planned Preventative Maintenance (PPM) schedules
  • GG03 - Root Cause Analysis (RCA) investigation & reporting
  • GG05 - Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) implementation
  • GG06 - Work Instruction (WI) implementation
  • GG07 - Management of Change (MOC) implementation
