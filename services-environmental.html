Environmental & Sustainability

Environmental control aligned with ISO 14001 and client expectations

Overview

Environmental compliance is increasingly integral to operational licensing and tender requirements. We help clients identify environmental risks, meet legislative obligations, and demonstrate responsible practice. Our environmental consultancy services cover environmental risk assessment, pollution prevention, waste management, and ISO 14001-aligned procedures.

Effective environmental management protects your organisation from regulatory enforcement, reputational damage, and operational disruption. It also demonstrates corporate responsibility and can provide competitive advantage in tender processes where environmental credentials are assessed.

Consultancy Services Available

We conduct environmental risk assessments that identify potential impacts to air, water, soil, and ecosystems. Our documentation supports permit applications, environmental audits, and ongoing compliance monitoring. We help clients understand their obligations under environmental legislation including the Environmental Protection Act, Waste Duty of Care, and Environmental Permitting Regulations.

Our environmental service also includes spill response planning, waste management procedures, and pollution prevention plans. We work with site teams to develop practical controls that prevent environmental incidents and ensure rapid, effective response when issues do occur.

What We Provide

We work with your environmental and site management teams to develop practical environmental controls and management systems. Our documentation supports ISO 14001 certification, permit applications, and client audits.

From resource efficiency planning to contaminated land assessment, we deliver documentation that demonstrates environmental responsibility while remaining practical for implementation.

  • GE02 - Waste management, storage & disposal
